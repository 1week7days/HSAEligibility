# Sources

Use primary government sources where possible. Consumer-facing references are acceptable for practical category interpretation, but should not be copied wholesale.

## Primary Sources

- IRS Publication 502, Medical and Dental Expenses: https://www.irs.gov/publications/p502
- IRS Publication 969, Health Savings Accounts and Other Tax-Favored Health Plans: https://www.irs.gov/publications/p969
- IRS Topic No. 502, Medical and Dental Expenses: https://www.irs.gov/taxtopics/tc502

## Consumer-Facing References

- FSA Store Eligibility List: https://fsastore.com/fsa-eligibility-list
- HSA Store Eligibility List: https://hsastore.com/hsa-eligibility-list

## Curation Rules

- Prefer IRS sources for direct eligibility rules and examples.
- Include `sourceURL` and `lastUpdated` on every JSON item.
- Keep notes short, factual, and written in this repository's own wording.
- Do not copy proprietary HSA Store or FSA Store catalog text wholesale.
- Mark conditional items as `lmn_required`, `prescription_required`, or `uncertain` instead of forcing them into `eligible`.
